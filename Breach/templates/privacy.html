Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2025

At BreachXpress, we are committed to protecting your privacy and fostering transparency in our mission to expose corruption and empower whistleblowers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information submitted through our website at www.breachxpress.com (the "Website"). By using the Website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

BreachXpress collects minimal information, limited to user-submitted stories, evidence files, and contact form submissions. We prioritize anonymity for whistleblowers and do not store personal data unless voluntarily provided. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@breachxpress.com.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User-Submitted Stories and Evidence

When you submit a story or evidence file through our "Submit Your Story" feature, we collect:

  • The content of your story, including title, summary, and narrative.
  • Optional evidence files (e.g., PDF, JPG, PNG, DOC) you choose to upload.
  • Optional metadata, such as category and tags, to categorize your submission.

We do not require or collect personal information (e.g., name, email) for story submissions unless you voluntarily include it within the content. Submissions are designed to be anonymous to protect whistleblowers.

1.2 Contact Form Submissions

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name (optional).
  • Your email address (required).
  • The subject and message of your inquiry.

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not used for marketing or other purposes.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics tools to collect personal information. However, our servers may automatically log non-personal information, such as:

  • Your IP address (anonymized where possible).
  • Browser type and version.
  • Pages visited and timestamps.

This information is used for security, troubleshooting, and improving the Website’s functionality and is not linked to identifiable individuals.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Review, verify, and publish user-submitted stories and evidence as exposés, in alignment with our mission to expose corruption.
  • Respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the Website (e.g., detecting and preventing malicious activity).
  • Comply with legal obligations, such as responding to lawful requests from authorities.

3. Anonymity and Confidentiality

BreachXpress is dedicated to protecting the anonymity of whistleblowers and submitters. We employ technical and organizational measures, such as secure data storage and anonymization of submissions, to safeguard your identity. For example:

  • Story submissions do not require personal information.
  • Evidence files are stored securely and stripped of identifiable metadata where possible.
  • Contact form data is encrypted during transmission and stored securely.

However, anonymity cannot be guaranteed in all cases, such as when we are legally required to disclose information (e.g., via court order). By submitting content, you acknowledge these limitations.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store submitted stories, evidence files, and contact form data on secure servers located in India. We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain information as follows:

  • Stories and Evidence: Published exposés and their evidence are retained indefinitely to maintain a public record of corruption, unless removed for legal or ethical reasons. Unpublished submissions are retained for up to one year for review purposes, after which they may be deleted.
  • Contact Form Submissions: We retain contact form data for up to one year to ensure we can respond to inquiries, after which it is deleted unless required for legal purposes.
  • Automatically Collected Information: Server logs are retained for up to 90 days for security and troubleshooting, then deleted.

If you wish to request deletion of your submission or contact data, please contact us at contact@breachxpress.com. Note that published exposés may remain public to serve our mission.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Publication: Approved stories and evidence may be published as exposés on the Website, accessible to the public. Personal information, if included, is anonymized unless you explicitly consent to its inclusion.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information to comply with legal requirements, such as responding to court orders or government requests.
  • Service Providers: We may use trusted third-party providers (e.g., hosting services) to store or process data on our behalf. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

7. International Users

The Website is operated from India and intended for a global audience. If you are located outside India, your information may be transferred to and stored in India, where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction. By using the Website, you consent to this transfer.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) by:

  • Collecting minimal data and prioritizing anonymity.
  • Providing clear information about data use (this Privacy Policy).
  • Offering the right to request deletion of your submissions (subject to our retention policy).

If you have GDPR-related concerns, contact us at contact@breachxpress.com.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Website is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has submitted information, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information should contact us at contact@breachxpress.com.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request details about the information we hold about you (e.g., contact form submissions).
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your submissions or contact data, subject to our retention policy.
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information (though our minimal data collection limits such scenarios).

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@breachxpress.com. We will respond within 30 days, in accordance with applicable laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@breachxpress.com
  • Address: 123, Justice Street, Truth City, India
