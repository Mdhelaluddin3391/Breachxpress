Frequently Asked Questions

How does BreachXpress protect whistleblowers?

We prioritize anonymity with secure submission systems, encrypted storage, and metadata removal. While we strive to protect your identity, legal obligations may apply. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What types of stories can I submit?

We welcome stories about corruption, injustice, or misconduct, supported by evidence like documents or photos. Submissions are reviewed for accuracy and impact.

How can I join the BreachXpress community?

Visit our Community page to engage with truth-seekers, share insights, and amplify our exposés.
